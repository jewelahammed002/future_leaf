Enjoy the TEA and improve your health conditions.

Recent research including animal studies shows that tea drinking may significantly lower the risk for serious heart disease including heart attack and blood clots.

Green tea contains a small amount of caffeine that can help increase energy. The low caffeine amounts make green tea a good choice for people who are looking to cut back on their caffeine intake.

The potential health benefits of tea go far beyond simply improving immune system health. Drink tea daily to prevent the onset of neurological decline and help protect heart health.

About

Tea leaves are small to medium in size and oval to elliptical in shape, averaging 5-10 centimeters in length. The shiny dark green leaves grow in an alternate pattern, are leathery in texture, and have serrated edges that taper to a pointed tip.
